Nature Preserve Simulator

A top-down nature preservation simulator where the player is in charge of a randomly generated wild envrionment from an omnipresent view. They are tasked with developing this environment growing plants, cultivating and helping it flurish with animals and life

Overview

This will be a top down game where the players create a randomly generated world with their own seed they can randomly get or select this will randomly generate an enviroment using perlin noise creating trees water grass and other enviroment objects. They will then be able to zoom in and look around and interact with the envrionment in a multitude differnet ways. The first one of these ways of cultivaiton is harvesting the already growing plants and creating grow areas so that different plants can flurish and even be cross-bred into new ones. Doing this can open a multitude of options for the player to evolve their environemnt
