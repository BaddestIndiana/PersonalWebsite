Phishing

A HTML based fishing game where the player controls a lure and must catch as many fish as they can with different mechanics and gameplay elements

In this game the player plays as a fishermans' lure that is cast into the sea and they must avoid fish that are trying to get caught. When the player hits a fish they start being pulled back up and must catch all the fish they were just avoiding to gain as many points as they can until they are at the surface. This is a simple idea that I have put a spin on and plays abit like an old bullet hell like game where the main gameplay loop is competing with people you know to get the highest score on the leaderboard.

Scripts

The Scripts that were created for the game were very intresting and enjoyable to make as they were very game specific scripts with the game being so unique in its gameplay and mechanics. These included sctipts that would handle simulating the depth of the sea as the player casts into it, this was achieved by a counter that uses the Time.DeltaTime function to count how long the player had been sinking, it then used this data to print a depth value to the screen and also trigger different events such as the fish and backgorunds to change at lower depths, to give a visual hint and make it harder for the player as the descend. This script also worked to do the opposite as the player was ascending back to the surface, counting down instead and changing the fish and backgorunds back.
This script also allowed for the bubble spawner and fish spawner to switch depending on if the player was moving up through the water or down into it. This was just one of the few important scripts used in this project and I worked to make sure these were efficient, effective and fitting for the HTML build.

Art and Visuals

For the visuals I decided to keep it simple and go with a pixel art style as I felt that it fit the very arcade like gameplay where the players objective is to get the highest score on the leaderboard. This can be seen with my choice of exaggerated sprites and bright colours that lend to this and I believe suit the game well.
