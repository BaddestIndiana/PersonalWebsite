Rouge-Like

A Top-Down Rouge Like based in the a Medieval Japan Where Players are Casted into the Depths of Hell and Must Fight Through Japanese Demons and Folk-Law Enemies and Collect Items to Upgrade their Characters

Overview

The game first starts by selecting character from a choice of three, them being the ranger, swords man and mage they then give them a name. The player is then asked to start the game either solo or with a gorup up to four once decided they are spawned into the first floor and faced with a small room with three doors. the players must pick a direction to travel in and then progress through ten randomly generated rooms to reach a boss room that will lead them to the stairs to the floor below. while they go they will encounter a range of different equipment they will have to swap out and manage to put up a fight in the increasing difficulty. The idea is that the characters will become special and refind the more they progress gaining rare items and traits. If the player dies however there character will be put into a player graveyard where they can see themselves and there past progress. This will later play into gameplay as you will be forced to fight your past characters to progress. Art and Visuals

The art and visuals for the game are going to take a pixel art look with bright colours to put a spin on the demonic themes of old japanese folk-law. This will give the game a good look and easily translate visually whats going on while giving something nice to look at. For this I have my friend contributing his artistic skills while I help to direct him to my vision while also letting him have his own creative freedom. for these sprites we are gonna use 64 by 64 pixel sprite sheets so that the sprites will be detailed enough while also achiving that pixel art style we are creating. His art will aslo be shown on the Jira page so that we can keep track of the sprites made and yet to be made and work around each other as we will know what we are both working on at any point in time.
